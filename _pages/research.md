## Research Areas

### Refrigeration and Cooling Systems
Design and optimization of refrigeration cycles, cooling circuits, and heat exchangers for domestic appliances.

### Thermal Engineering
Heat transfer analysis, thermal insulation evaluation, energy efficiency improvement, and thermal management.

### Embedded Control Systems
Development and implementation of intelligent control logic for refrigerator operation, sensor integration, and performance optimization.

### Industrial IoT
Connected appliances, remote monitoring, data acquisition, and smart energy management systems.

### Intelligent Decision-Making Systems
Application of optimization, machine learning, and control strategies for autonomous engineering systems.
