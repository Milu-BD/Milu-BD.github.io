## Project

**Fabrication and Performance Analysis of an IoT-Based Smart Solar PV-Panel Tracking System**

This project was completed as my undergraduate thesis in Mechanical Engineering at Rajshahi University of Engineering & Technology (RUET). The work involved the design, fabrication, implementation, and performance evaluation of an intelligent solar photovoltaic (PV) panel tracking system integrated with Internet of Things (IoT) technology.

## Problem

Conventional fixed solar panels cannot continuously maintain an optimal orientation toward the sun throughout the day. As a result, solar energy harvesting efficiency is reduced, leading to lower power generation and underutilization of available solar resources.

The challenge was to develop a cost-effective and intelligent system capable of automatically tracking the sun's movement to maximize solar energy collection while providing remote monitoring capabilities.

## Method

* Designed a solar tracking mechanism capable of adjusting panel orientation based on sunlight position.
* Developed an embedded control system to automate tracking operations.
* Integrated IoT technology for remote monitoring and data acquisition.
* Fabricated a prototype system and conducted experimental testing under real operating conditions.
* Collected and analyzed exergy to compare the energy output of the tracking system with that of a fixed solar panel setup.

## Tools

* Arduino Microcontroller
* IoT Communication Modules
* Solar Photovoltaic Panels
* Thermal Sensors and Actuators
* Embedded Programming
* Solidworks Design for Fabrication
* Data Collection and Analysis
* Engineering Testing Instruments

## Results

* Successfully fabricated and implemented a functional IoT-based solar PV tracking system.
* Achieved automatic solar tracking throughout daylight hours.
* Enabled remote monitoring of system performance through IoT integration.
* Demonstrated improved solar energy harvesting compared to conventional fixed-panel installations.
* Validated the feasibility of integrating embedded control and IoT technologies into renewable energy systems for enhanced efficiency and intelligent operation.
