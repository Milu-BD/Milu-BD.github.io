## Project

**Refrigerator Cooling Circuit Design and Thermal Performance Optimization**

As a Refrigeration Systems Design & Control Engineer at Walton Hi-Tech Industries PLC., I am involved in the design, analysis, testing, and optimization of refrigerator cooling systems to improve performance, reliability, safety, and energy efficiency.

## Problem

Modern refrigeration systems must achieve rapid cooling performance while minimizing energy consumption and ensuring long-term reliability. Designing an optimized cooling circuit requires balancing multiple factors, including cooling capacity, refrigerant flow characteristics, compressor performance, heat transfer efficiency, and regulatory safety requirements.

The challenge was to enhance refrigeration system performance while maintaining product quality, cost-effectiveness, and compliance with industry and market standards.

## Method

* Developed and evaluated refrigeration cooling circuit configurations.
* Conducted thermal performance testing under various operating conditions.
* Analyzed refrigeration cycle parameters and system behavior.
* Performed cooling performance and energy consumption assessments.
* Investigated reliability and safety characteristics of refrigeration systems.
* Collaborated with cross-functional teams to validate design improvements and optimize system performance.
